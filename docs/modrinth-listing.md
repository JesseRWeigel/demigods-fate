## Description (Markdown)

# Demigod's Fate

**A Percy Jackson RPG mod** for Minecraft 1.21.1. Become a demigod, get claimed by an Olympian god, and adventure through the world of Greek and Roman mythology.

Inspired by Rick Riordan's *Percy Jackson & the Olympians*, *Heroes of Olympus*, and *Trials of Apollo* book series.

---

## Choose Your God-Parent

Pick from **13 Olympian gods** — 7 Greek and 6 Roman. Each gives you unique passive abilities and 3 cooldown powers bound to R, V, G keys.

| Greek | Roman | Powers |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **Poseidon** | Neptune | Water breathing, Water Blast, Earthquake, Hurricane Form |
| **Zeus** | Jupiter | No fall damage, Lightning Strike, Wind Gust, Storm Call |
| **Athena** | Minerva | See mob health, Battle Plan, Aegis Aura, Tactical Strike |
| **Ares** | Mars | +30% damage, Bloodrage, War Cry, Weapon Mastery |
| **Hephaestus** | — | Fire immunity, Forge Fire, Mechanical Trap, Bronze Automaton |
| **Apollo** | — | Arrow boost, Healing Hymn, Sun Arrow, Plague Shot |
| **Hermes** | — | +20% speed, Shadow Step, Steal, Swiftness |
| — | **Pluto** | See ores, Raise Dead, Shadow Meld, Death Grasp |
| — | **Bellona** | Sword boost, War Fury, Rally, Siege Breaker |

Don't like your choice? Visit the Oracle for a **rebirth** — switch gods for 50 drachmas!

## Fight Mythological Monsters

**8 custom monsters** with unique mechanics:

- **Minotaur** — Charges in straight lines for devastating damage
- **Medusa** — DON'T LOOK AT HER! Stone Gaze petrifies you. Use a shield to reflect it back!
- **Hydra** — Cut one head, two grow back! Must use FIRE to kill (abilities, Greek Fire, lava)
- **Chimera** — Fire breath + poison tail
- **Hellhound** — Shadow teleports behind you
- **Fury** — Fire whip, pursues for 48 blocks
- **Empousai** — Charmspeak stun, teleports away when low
- **Cyclops** — Boulder throw from range

## Wield Divine Weapons

**8 legendary weapons** with god-affinity bonuses:

- **Riptide** — Percy's sword. Unbreakable. +50% damage near water (Poseidon)
- **Aegis Shield** — Medusa's face terrifies nearby mobs. Fear pushback (Zeus/Athena)
- **Ivlivs** — Jason's coin-sword. 25% lightning on hit (Jupiter)
- **Backbiter** — Luke's cursed blade. Damages everything. Steal gold (Hermes)
- **Stygian Iron Sword** — Life drain. Prevents monster respawn (Pluto)
- **Leo's Forge Hammer** — Sets mobs on fire. Fire ring on hit (Hephaestus)
- **Katoptris** — Reveals enemies through walls (Aphrodite)
- **Morphing Spear** — Frank's shape-shifting weapon. +20% in all forms (Mars)

**4 weapon material tiers:** Celestial Bronze → Imperial Gold → Stygian Iron → Bone Steel

## Explore Custom Dimensions

- **The Underworld** — Dark soul sand caverns beneath the world
- **Mount Olympus** — Golden palaces floating above the clouds
- **The Labyrinth** — Procedurally generated maze that changes EVERY entry! 6 room types including Daedalus's Workshop

## Build Your Camp

Use `/build camp_halfblood` or `/build camp_jupiter` to generate a full camp:

- **Camp Half-Blood**: 12 themed cabins (Zeus=marble, Poseidon=prismarine, Hades=obsidian...), Big House with Chiron & Mr. D NPCs, amphitheater campfire
- **Camp Jupiter**: 5 cohort barracks, Principia HQ, Senate House, Temple Hill, Little Tiber river

Camps are **safe zones** — no hostile mob spawning inside!

## Prophecy-Driven Quests

The Oracle delivers cryptic 4-line prophecies, and you follow them across the world:

**The Lightning Thief** — 9-stage questline following the first book:
1. Fight the Minotaur at the camp border
2. Train at camp
3. Receive the Oracle's prophecy
4. Aunty Em's Garden (Medusa boss)
5. Gateway Arch (Chimera)
6. Lotus Casino (time-trap puzzle)
7. The Underworld (Cerberus, Hades)
8. Beach Showdown (Ares boss)
9. Mount Olympus (deliver the bolt to Zeus)

Plus **6 repeatable monster bounties** for drachma rewards!

## Items & Economy

- **Golden Drachmas** — Currency dropped by monsters, used for Oracle services
- **Ambrosia** — Heals 8 hearts (max 3/day or you burn!)
- **Nectar** — Regeneration IV for 30 seconds
- **Greek Fire** — Throwable green fire that burns through anything
- **Hermes Multivitamins** — Clears all negative effects
